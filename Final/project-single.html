Weather Shadowbox

Interaction Design

Inspired by the Weasley's clock that showed where family memebers where, I wanted to create a analog display of something very numeric like weather conditions. I created a weather shadow box to do this.

  • Weather Shadowbox: concept sketch

Concept based on the mixing of "the magic of analog motion" with the power of IOT (intertnet of things).

  • Weather Shadowbox: Cardboard mockup

To rough out the idea and test different feasibilities.

  • Weather Shadowbox: Cardboard mockup

Hand drawn illustrations of weather events is part of the analog aesthetic.

  • Weather Shadowbox: Cardboard mockup

The cities are displayed on drum that rotates into view. In this case its a medice bottle.

  • Weather Shadowbox: Foamcore Model

Constructed from foamcore; the tolerances and rigidity fo the material allow for better placement of servos and swing arms.

Designed to hang on a wall the mechanism will only partially be hidden.
